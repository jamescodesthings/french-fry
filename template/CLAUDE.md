### Recommended workflow

1. **French Fry** for task breakdown and delegation
2. **Haggis** or **James** for implementation (Haggis leads, James supports)
3. **Iris** for UX/design review before shipping
4. **Katie** for compliance/risk review before shipping
5. **Roo** for a raw gut-check at any point
